About the Inn

A calm retreat rooted in Sri Lanka's heritage

Nestled along Nawapadeniya Road in Dambulla, Rangiri Heritage Inn is a lovingly appointed guesthouse offering six individually styled rooms with every modern comfort.

Wake to birdsong, explore millennia-old temples, and return to a home that feels both timeless and utterly welcoming.

  • 6 rooms — all with hot water, TV & AC
  • Expert guidance to heritage sites & national parks
  • Vehicle arrangements for Sigiriya & beyond
  • Traditional Sri Lankan breakfast available
